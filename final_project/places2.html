Places

Where my favorite places are where:

Places where my family are:

South Africa: my mom's side of the family lives here so I have many fond memories with them here. I also really enjoy going on the safari rides.

England: My dad's side of the family lives in England. Similar to South Africa, I also have many fond memories here. England's rich history adds to its appeal

Places where I have happy memories:

Germany: I went to Germany over winter break 2019. On this trip I saw snow for the first time. Germany has such rich culture so it was really enriching to learn more and experience it first hand. This was also the last trip before COVID happened.

New York City: In my first semester of college I visited my sister in NYC. I felt very overwhelmed by college and moving to a new country but seeing her made me feel happy and at home. Before this trip, I had already been to nyc before. The city is an exciting place to be and has something for every occasion. I also really enjoy how easy it is to walk everywhere.

Places where I feel at home:

Taiwan: I have spent the majority of my life in Taiwan and have family here. I lived here since I was 10. The people here are some of the most respectful and kind people I have met, building my values.

USC: Despite feeling very foreign at first, after spending a whole year on campus I have grown to love the campus. The campus is very pretty and I have created friends that feel like my family now.

All the places I have lived and traveled to have shaped me into the person I am today. I love to travel and explore and would love to live all over the world.
