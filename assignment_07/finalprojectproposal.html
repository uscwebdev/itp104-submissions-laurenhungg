Topic: People and Places I Love

For my final project, I want to create a website dedicated to the places and people I cherish the most. From places in my home country, Taiwan, to places my family frequently visits for holidays.

Audience:

My website's audience is those that I am closest to and also myself. I think it would be fun to look back on a website showcasing all my favorite places and people.

Design and Style:

I want to have a minimalistic but colorful website, meaning I will have a simple design and font but use different colors.

Color Palette:

  • Primary colors - off white (#f6f5f0), powder (#f0e4d8)
  • Secondary colors - mauve pink (#d7b3b6), yellow (#eedc82), blue (#798fa8)
  • Text color: black
